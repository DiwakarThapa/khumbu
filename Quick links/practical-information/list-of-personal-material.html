Your equipment for a trek or an expedition

Do not look for tents, foam mattresses, plates, forks, bowls, stoves and other utensils, we take care of them.
The equipment for the trek is the three-layer set. The first layer on the body to wick away perspiration, a second warm thermal layer and a windbreaker.
The day during the trek you have to worry about your personal belongings a small bag of 35-45 liters is enough with inside a gourd, a windbreaker, hat-cap, gloves, glasses, a polar, some racing supplies, a spare thermal underwear, a survival blanket and the camera.
The rest of your business will be placed in a bag of about 70-100 liters for a trek or trek peak and 100 to 140 liters for an expedition (this is the bag that you put in the hold on the plane) that will be entrusted to a carrier during the trek.
Do not skimp on the quality of the sleeping bag even for a trek. It is the temperature of comfort that must be taken into account.
