Trekking in Khumbu



Three treks in Khumbu valley of Everest

Everest in panoramic where the earth meets the sky in Sagarmatha

These 3 trekking in Khumbu, transport us to the heart of the Everest massif in Sherpas country . Khumbu is the most beautiful mountains in the world, Ama Dablam, Everest, Nuptse, Pumori, etc. CNN ranks the Khumbu Everest Base Camp Kala Patthar trek as the most beautiful adventure trek.The Khumbu region is home to Sagarmatha Park which is a 1,200-square kilometer protected area bordering the Tibet Nature Reserve.Fir trees, rhododendron forests, suspension bridges, yak caravans, Sherpa villages, you are in Khumbu. From Namche Bazar you are at high altitude with the view of the prestigious peaks of Nepal. It is also many Buddhist monasteries like the oldest Pangboche of Khumbu and Tengboche which forms Lamas. Sagarmatha is also a World Heritage Park with a rich flora and fauna. The trek of the high passes offers the Renjo Pass , the Chola Pass and then we will go to Kala Patthar La. A trip to the heart of the Everest massif by the high passes. In Khumbu we are still above 3500 m with many villages over 4000 m.Sunrise and sunset on Everest as you ascend to Kala Patthar are unforgettable moments like visiting Base Camp Everest.The High Route from Everest to Nangpa The 5700 m is poorly done. It is a difficult trek which is done partly under tent. It is the historic road between Tibet and Khumbu frequented by Tibetans who trade between Nepal and Tibet via the Nangpa La Glacier.


Highlights of Khumbu treks

  • In the heart of the most famous and highest mountains on the planet
  • Passing the passes more than 5000 m
  • Being on the border with Tibet at Nangpa La with the view of Cho Oyu 8200 m on the High Route of Everest
  • The climb to Gokyo Peak offers great views of the entire Khumbu Range and the Gokyo Lakes
  • What a great reward to see the sunset or sunrise on Everest from Kala Patthar (especially sunset for colors)
  • Sherpa Country and Namche Bazar Market
  • Comfortable lodges
