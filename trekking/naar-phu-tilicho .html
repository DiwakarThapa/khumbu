Naar Phu Tilicho and Naar Thorong Pass Treks





Annapurna Tower by Naar Phu Tilicho and Naar Thorong Pass

Cultures in terraces, villages of another time, lake, under the look of the Annapurna


These treks Naar Phu Tilicho and Naar Thorong Pass take us on an original Annapurna Tour. We cross splendid landscapes, of a great diversity and fascinating.

From the lower Annapurnas valley with the green rice fields,then travel "in another time" in the valleys of Naar and Phu where one has the impression of being in another world

then to Kangla Pass One of the most beautiful views of the Annapurna Range and it continues by the mineral route of the climb to Lake Tilicho , to the snowy peaks of Annapurna and Tilicho
to finish by crossing the Mesokanto Pass with a descent into a desert environment on Jomson.


Be it the circuit by the Tilicho lake or the Thorong Pass in both cases we are well acclimated to the altitude because we have already passed the Kangla La at 5200 m and we had a rest day in Manang.
Yes this circuit is exceptional. To use the expression of a trekker during this journey "it's even more beautiful than I thought".

Highlights of Naar Phu Tilicho and Thorong Pass
  • The diversity of the landscapes the rice fields at the beginning, Naar Phu typed Tibetan, then the mineral aspect of the ascent to the Tilicho lake
  • The village of Phu lost and anchored in the mountain far from everything
  • The inhabitants of the village of Naar
  • The view from Kangla La all over the Annapurna range
  • The bypass of Tilicho Lake
  • The passage of the Mesokanto pass and the descent to Jomson.
  • Arrival at Thorong Pass
